About Alterra Landscaping

We are a full-service landscaping company that services the local communities of Toronto and Greater Toronto Area and we offer a number of services for both residential and business. Whether you are needing a new interlocking driveway and sidewalk work or looking to add a new deck, fences, sodding and plants.

We provide a wide range of landscaping services to hundreds of customers. We employ a total of 10 highly trained individuals who are only interested in providing all of our customers with only the best possible service at a reasonable price. No job is too small or too complex, we can handle whatever landscaping you are needing to be done.

Our Services

Interlocking

Interlocking driveways, patios, walkways. We specialize in laying down a unique looking interlocking using a number of different pavers to create a decorative pattern that will look much better than plain concrete or asphalt. We have a number of patterns for you to choose from.

Fences

We can install a number of different types of fencing including; chain link (in a variety of heights), wooden stockade fencing. We can replace an existing fence and install or make repairs to the existing fencing.

Decks

We can install a great new deck off the back door or adjacent to above ground backyard pool. We offer to install any deck in a number of different sizes, materials and installation methods in order.

Sodding and Planting

We offer a full line of landscaping services including complete resodding of the yard (front and back), repair small areas. We can put in flowers, bushes, trees and shrubs in any size flower beds custom fit to your home and yard configuration.
